‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What's the minimum budget needed to start with AI tools?

You can start with essential AI tools for as little as $47/month (ChatGPT + Canva + Grammarly). This provides immediate value in content creation and communication.

How long does it take to see ROI from AI tools?

Most businesses see positive ROI within 30-60 days. Content creation tools show immediate impact, while automation tools may take 2-3 months to fully optimize.

Which AI tool provides the highest ROI for small businesses?

ChatGPT consistently provides the highest ROI due to its versatility in content creation, customer service, and business strategy. Many businesses save 10-15 hours per week.

Are free AI tools good enough for small businesses?

Free tools are great for testing, but paid versions offer better features, higher usage limits, and business-specific functionality that justify the investment.

How do I choose the right AI tools for my business?

Start with your biggest pain points. If it's content creation, start with ChatGPT. If it's customer service, try Intercom. Focus on solving one problem at a time.
