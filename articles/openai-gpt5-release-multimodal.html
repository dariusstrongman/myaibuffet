OpenAI has officially unveiled GPT-5, marking the most significant advancement in artificial intelligence since the original ChatGPT launch. The new model demonstrates unprecedented multimodal capabilities, seamlessly integrating text, image, audio, and video processing in a single, unified system.

Early benchmarks reveal that GPT-5 shows a remarkable 40% improvement over GPT-4 across multiple evaluation metrics, with particularly strong performance in reasoning, mathematical problem-solving, and creative tasks.
